Stage 1 of 4 · On your phone or laptop
Create Your Stremio Account
Takes about 2 minutes. Do this on your phone or laptop — not the TV. Everything syncs to the TV automatically once you log in.
1✓
Go to the Stremio website
Open your phone or laptop browser and head to stremio.com.

→ Open stremio.com
2✓
Click Sign Up and create an account
Enter your email address and choose a password. You'll get a confirmation email — click the link in it to verify your account.

✍️ Write it down! You'll need your Stremio email and password when you log into the TV later.
3✓
Download Stremio on your phone or laptop
Download the app for your device, open it, and log in with the account you just made.

→ Download Stremio
Stage 2 of 4 · On your phone or laptop
Set Up Real-Debrid
Real-Debrid is a small paid service (~€3/month) that makes everything faster, smoother, and safer. Without it, streams can be slow or buffery. It's worth it.
🛡️ Why Real-Debrid?
Instead of downloading from other people's computers (which can be slow or risky), Real-Debrid has already saved popular content on their own fast servers. You stream directly from them — much quicker, much safer, and no dodgy connections.
1✓
Go to Real-Debrid and create an account
Click the link below, then click Sign Up. Enter a username, your email, and a password. Verify your email when they send you a confirmation.

→ Open Real-Debrid
✍️ Write this down too! Keep your Real-Debrid login separate from your Stremio one.
2✓
Choose a plan and pay
Once logged in, go to the Premium Offers page. Here's what the plans look like in AUD:

  1. 15 days — ~$4.93 AUD · Good if you just want to try it
  2. 30 days — ~$6.58 AUD
  3. 90 days — ~$14.79 AUD ← Start here
  4. 180 days — ~$26.30 AUD ← Switch to this after
💡 Recommendation: Start with the 90-day plan to try it out. Once you're happy with it, grab the 180-day plan next time — best value at about $4.40/month AUD.

It's prepaid, not a subscription — so every 90 or 180 days you just log back in and pay again. No automatic charges.
→ View Premium Plans
✅ This is the only cost in the whole setup. Stremio and the addon are completely free.
3✓
Get your Real-Debrid API Key
Your API key is like a password that connects Real-Debrid to Stremio. Here's how to find it:

  1. While logged into Real-Debrid, click this link:
→ Get my API Key
  1. You'll see a long string of letters and numbers — that's your key
  2. Copy it (press and hold on mobile, or Ctrl+C / Cmd+C on laptop)
  3. Paste it somewhere handy — Notes app on your phone works great
🔑 Keep this key safe. Don't share it with anyone — it's linked to your paid account.
Stage 3 of 4 · On your phone or laptop
Install & Connect the Addon
This is where you connect Real-Debrid to Stremio using the API key you just copied. You only do this once — it'll sync to your TV automatically.
1✓
Open the Torrentio setup page
Torrentio is the addon that finds movies and shows for you. Open it on the same device where Stremio is installed.

→ Open Torrentio
2✓
Select Real-Debrid as your provider
On the Torrentio page, look for a dropdown that says Debrid provider (or similar). Click it and select Real-Debrid from the list.

A new field will appear below it asking for your API key. That's next.
3✓
Paste your Real-Debrid API key
In the API key field that appeared, paste the key you copied in Stage 2.

(It's the long string of letters and numbers you saved in your Notes app.)

✅ Once this is entered, all your streams will come through Real-Debrid — fast and safe.
4✓
Click Install and confirm in Stremio
Scroll down and click the big Install button. A popup will appear — click Open Stremio, then click Install to confirm.

Once Stremio says the addon is installed, you're done with this stage! 🎉
Stage 4 of 4 · On your Samsung TV
Install Stremio on Your Samsung TV
Grab your Samsung remote — this is the last step. Works on Samsung TVs from 2019 onwards.
1✓
Press the Home button on your remote
It's the button that looks like a little house. This takes you to the Samsung home screen.
2✓
Go to Apps
Use the remote to navigate to Apps — it looks like a grid of squares, usually at the bottom of the home screen.
3✓
Search for Stremio and install it
Click the magnifying glass (search) icon and type Stremio. Select it from the results and click Install. Takes about a minute to download.
4✓
Open Stremio and log in
Open the app, select Log In, and enter the Stremio email and password you wrote down in Stage 1.

📝 Heads up: Use your Stremio login here — not your Samsung account or Real-Debrid one.
5✓
Search for something and watch!
Use the search bar to find a movie or TV show. Pick a stream near the top of the list — those are your Real-Debrid streams, which are the fastest. Click play and enjoy! 🎉

RD streams are labelled — look for ones that say RD next to them. These are the good ones.
🎉

You're all set, Chrissy!

Stremio is ready on your Samsung TV with Real-Debrid powering the streams. Fast, smooth, and safe — enjoy!

Quick Tips

Look for RD streams — in the stream list, pick ones marked RD. These come from Real-Debrid and are the fastest
Can't find a stream? Try searching with the year — e.g. Succession 2018
Buffering? Pick a different RD stream from the list — try the next one down
Very new episode missing? Give it a day or two for brand new episodes to appear
Something not working? Quick TV restart usually fixes it — or message Steve 😄
