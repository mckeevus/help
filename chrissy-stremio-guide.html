Stage 1 of 5 · On your phone or laptop
Create Your Stremio Account
Takes about 2 minutes. Do this on your phone or laptop — not the TV. Everything syncs to the TV automatically once you log in.
1✓
Go to the Stremio website
Open your phone or laptop browser and head to stremio.com.

→ Open stremio.com
2✓
Click Sign Up and create an account
Enter your email and choose a password. Verify your email when they send you a link.

✍️ Write it down! You'll need your Stremio email and password when you log into the TV later.
3✓
Download Stremio on your phone or laptop
Download the app, open it, and log in with the account you just made.

→ Download Stremio
Stage 2 of 5 · On your phone or laptop
Set Up Real-Debrid
Real-Debrid is a small paid service that makes everything faster, smoother, and safer. Without it, streams can be slow or buffery. It's worth it.
🛡️ Why Real-Debrid?
Instead of streaming from random people's computers, Real-Debrid has already saved popular content on their own fast servers. You stream directly from them — much quicker, much safer.
1✓
Go to Real-Debrid and create an account
Click Sign Up, enter a username, email, and password. Verify your email.

→ Open Real-Debrid
✍️ Write this down separately from your Stremio login.
2✓
Choose a plan and pay
Go to Premium Offers. Here's what the plans cost in AUD:

  1. 15 days — ~$4.93 AUD · Just want to try it
  2. 30 days — ~$6.58 AUD
  3. 90 days — ~$14.79 AUD ← Start here
  4. 180 days — ~$26.30 AUD ← Switch to this after
💡 Recommendation: Start with 90 days. Once you're happy, grab 180 days next time — about $4.40/month AUD.

It's prepaid, not a subscription — every 90 or 180 days you just log back in and pay again. No automatic charges.
→ View Premium Plans
✅ This is the only cost in the whole setup. Stremio and the addons are completely free.
3✓
Get your Real-Debrid API Key
Your API key connects Real-Debrid to Stremio. Here's how to get it:

  1. Click the link below while logged into Real-Debrid
  2. You'll see a long string of letters and numbers — that's your key
  3. Copy it and paste it into your Notes app to keep it handy
→ Get my API Key
🔑 Keep this key safe. Don't share it — it's linked to your paid account.
Stage 3 of 5 · On your phone or laptop
Install & Configure the Addon
Connect Real-Debrid to Stremio and set the quality filters so you only see good streams. You only do this once.
1✓
Open the Torrentio setup page
Open this on the same device where Stremio is installed.

→ Open Torrentio
2✓
Select Real-Debrid as your provider
Find the Debrid provider dropdown and select Real-Debrid. A new field will appear for your API key — paste it in there.
Your API key is the long string you copied into your Notes app in Stage 2.
3✓
Set your quality filters
Scroll down to Exclude Resolutions. You want to exclude the bad/extreme quality options and keep only 4K, 1080p, 720p and good quality rips.

4✓
Click Install and confirm in Stremio
Scroll down and click the big Install button. When the popup appears, click Open Stremio then confirm the installation.

✅ Done! Your streams will now only show quality options via Real-Debrid.
Stage 4 of 5 · On your Samsung TV
Install Stremio on Your Samsung TV
Grab your Samsung remote. Works on Samsung TVs from 2019 onwards.
1✓
Press the Home button on your remote
The little house button. Takes you to the Samsung home screen.
2✓
Go to Apps
Navigate to Apps — the grid of squares at the bottom of the home screen.
3✓
Search for Stremio and install it
Click the search icon and type Stremio. Select it and click Install. Takes about a minute.
4✓
Open Stremio and log in
Open the app, select Log In, and enter your Stremio email and password from Stage 1.

📝 Heads up: Use your Stremio login — not your Samsung or Real-Debrid one.
5✓
Search for something and watch!
Use the search bar to find a movie or show. Pick a stream marked RD — those are your Real-Debrid streams, the fastest and safest. Enjoy! 🎉
Stage 5 of 5 · On your phone or laptop
Final Setup
A few finishing touches — remove the junk addons Stremio pre-installs, add streaming catalogues so you can browse by service, and set up a kids channel for little ones.
1✓
Remove the pre-installed addons
Stremio comes with some default addons that aren't useful and clutter things up. Remove these three:

▶️
YouTube
Watch YouTube channels in Stremio
Uninstall
WH
WatchHub
Shows where to buy/rent content — not needed
Uninstall
🎬
Public Domain Movies
Old movies from the 1920s–50s
Uninstall
How to remove them:
  1. In Stremio, click the 🧩 puzzle piece icon in the left sidebar (Addons)
  2. Find each addon listed above
  3. Click Uninstall next to each one
⚠️ Why remove WatchHub? It shows paid links to Apple TV, Amazon etc. alongside your free streams — don't accidentally click those and pay for something you can already watch for free!
2✓
Add streaming service catalogues
This addon adds browsable catalogues for Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, Apple TV+ and more — so you can see what's available on those platforms and watch it free through Stremio.

  1. Click the link below — it opens the catalogue addon setup page
  2. Click Install
  3. Confirm in Stremio when the popup appears
→ Install Streaming Catalogues
✅ Once installed, you'll see Netflix, Disney+, Prime and more appear in your Stremio home screen to browse.
3✓
Add Emily's Kids TV 🧒
A dedicated kids channel with safe, age-appropriate content — great if little ones are watching.

  1. Click the link below on your phone or laptop
  2. It will open Stremio and ask you to install the addon
  3. Click Install to confirm
  4. It will appear in your Stremio home screen as its own section

Quick Tips

Look for RD streams — in the stream list, pick ones marked RD. These are the fastest and safest
Can't find a stream? Try searching with the year — e.g. Succession 2018
Buffering? Pick a different RD stream from the list
Very new episode missing? Give it a day or two
Something not working? Quick TV restart usually fixes it — or message Steve 😄
