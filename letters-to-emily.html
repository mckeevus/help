Letters to Emily

You build things for everyone. Guides, dashboards, systems — utility, polished, handed over. This is the one build that outlasts all of them.

Not a journal. Not a task with a deadline. A set of letters Emily opens later — at the doorways you might not be standing in when she reaches them. Her 18th. The night something breaks her heart. The day she's holding her own kid and suddenly understands everything you never said.

Write them when you've got something to say, not on a schedule. One paragraph counts. The point was never to finish — it's that they exist.
